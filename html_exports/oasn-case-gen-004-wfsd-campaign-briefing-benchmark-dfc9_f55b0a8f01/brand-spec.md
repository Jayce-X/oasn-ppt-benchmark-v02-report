## Layout Posture

- Use a clean white institutional canvas with strong color bands, not a commercial landing-page treatment.
- Keep headings bold, condensed, and high-contrast; body copy stays plain and accessible.
- Use orange for campaign emphasis, blue for public-health authority, and green for readiness/action.
- Use tables, numbered flows, and simple SVG diagrams as editable briefing objects.
- Photography in the toolkit is evidentiary; when not embedding images, replace with labeled evidence panels rather than decorative illustration.
