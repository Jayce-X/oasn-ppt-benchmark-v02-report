Positioning

The Pro story is intelligence plus creative capture.

Apple frames the lineup as the most advanced Pro iPhone family, built around a faster chip, personal intelligence, and new capture controls.

Personal intelligence

Writing Tools, notification summaries, Priority Messages, richer Siri, and privacy architecture via Private Cloud Compute.

Pro creation system

Camera Control, 48MP Fusion, 48MP Ultra Wide, 5x Telephoto on both models, 4K120 fps Dolby Vision, and studio-quality mics.

Hardware headroom

A18 Pro adds CPU, GPU, Neural Engine, memory bandwidth, ISP, thermal, and battery improvements behind the experience.

Apple Intelligence

Generative models, personal context, privacy controls.

The PDF positions Apple Intelligence as a personal intelligence system that understands context, works across apps, and protects user data.

Writing Tools rewrite, proofread, summarize, and adjust tone across places users write.

Notes and Phone can record, transcribe, and summarize audio, with call participants notified when recording starts.

Summaries, Priority Messages, and richer Siri help users prioritize and complete everyday tasks.

The silicon story is efficiency, graphics, and AI headroom.

Built with second-generation 3-nanometer technology and a new transistor architecture.

Displays + design

Larger screens, thinner borders, stronger materials.

The Pro models move to 6.3-inch and 6.9-inch Super Retina XDR displays, with Always-On and ProMotion technologies.

Thinnest borders of any Apple product, according to the PDF.

Titanium body is positioned as strong, lightweight, and industry-leading in durability.

Latest-generation Ceramic Shield is listed as 2x tougher than glass on any other smartphone.

Display measurement footnote in PDF notes actual viewable area is less05 / 09

Camera Control

A new control surface for capture and visual intelligence.

The control combines hardware gestures with software previews: click, light press, touch, slide, frame, and adjust.

Hardware stack

Tactile switch, high-precision force sensor, and capacitive sensor enable click, light-press, and touch interactions.

Composition controls

A new camera preview lets users frame shots and adjust zoom, exposure, or depth of field by sliding a finger.

Visual intelligence

Later, users can pull up place details, add event information, identify objects, or use Google and ChatGPT gateways.

Camera Control section, including later two-stage shutter and visual intelligence updates06 / 09
